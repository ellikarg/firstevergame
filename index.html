The Rules of the game

  • You will receive 4 random numbers between 1 and 9
  • You can use all operators, as well as brackets, available on the right
  • Drag and drop the numbers, operators and - if necessary - the brackets in their respectively same-colored fields in the equation
  • Play around putting the numbers and operators in an order so that the result of the equation will be 24!
  • You can use the brackets to prioritise an addition or subtraction, otherwise - as are the basic rules of mathematics - the multiplication and division will be calculated first
